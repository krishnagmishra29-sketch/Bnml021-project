India Pushes Ahead With Semi High-Speed Rail Expansion

Photo: Unsplash

Indian Railways has accelerated work on multiple semi high-speed corridors as part of a broader infrastructure modernisation push. Officials believe the projects could reduce travel time between major cities while improving long-distance connectivity.

The expansion includes signalling upgrades, redevelopment of stations and improvements in rail-track infrastructure across several routes. Transport analysts say better railway networks often influence tourism, trade and urban growth.

Public investment in transport infrastructure has increasingly become central to economic planning discussions over the last few years. Experts argue that faster rail systems could play an important role in reducing pressure on road and air travel in densely connected regions.
