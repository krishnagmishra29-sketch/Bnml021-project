AI Tools Are Quietly Changing How Newsrooms Operate

Photo: Unsplash

Artificial Intelligence is gradually becoming part of everyday newsroom operations across digital media organisations. Recommendation systems, audience analytics and automation tools are now being integrated into editorial workflows.

Media researchers believe AI-assisted systems can improve efficiency in repetitive tasks and help organisations process large volumes of information more quickly.

At the same time, concerns related to misinformation, editorial judgement and accountability continue to shape discussions around AI integration in journalism.
