India's Cricket Team Begins Preparations for Busy Season

Photo: Unsplash

India's cricket setup has entered an intensive preparation phase ahead of a demanding international season. Fitness camps, recovery programmes and tactical sessions are already underway at multiple venues.

Team management officials have indicated that workload management will remain a major priority as schedules continue to become more demanding across formats.

Sports analysts believe modern cricket increasingly depends on data analysis, recovery systems and squad rotation rather than only on-field performance.
